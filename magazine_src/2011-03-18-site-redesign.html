Two years ago, I finished my prior redesign of my site. About a year ago, I started a new redesign. And after months of works, it is done.

Okay, not done. There are a number of things left to do— I am missing a bunch of pages, and there are a lot of little details I haven't had time for. However, I managed to get to a point where I had most of the major bases covered.

A lot has changed since my previous version. Browsers have caught up to developers and designers— we no longer have to use images and hacks and Flash to get what we want. And since I work at a company at the forefront of this movement, it seemed wrong not update my site to take full advantage of all the new technology we have available to us.

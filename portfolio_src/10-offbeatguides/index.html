In our hyper-connected world, sometimes you just want to disconnect. Offbeat Guides augments traditional printed travel guides with custom data such as weather and events for when you'll be at your destination. A few days before your trip, you get a high quality printed travel guide with your name gracing the cover.

I lead development of the Offbeat Guides website, which let users create, customize, manage and track orders. A few days before the start of a trip, our automated system generated professional quality PDFs in InDesign and sent the files to the printer closest to the customer's location.

In August 2010, I joined Mozilla's Add-ons team as a web developer. Firefox is by far the most customizable browser, largely due to its massive add-on collection. AMO (addons.mozilla.org) is responsible for hosting extensions, themes, dictionaries, search engines and plugins for Mozilla Firefox, Mozilla Thunderbird and SeaMonkey. The site is completely open source.

When I began, the team was in the process of a full rewrite of the site (from CakePHP to Django). The add-ons site now serves about a billion requests a day, making it the second largest Django project on the Internet.

In 2012, I switched roles to Add-ons Product Manager.
